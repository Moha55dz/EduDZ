EduDZ is a comprehensive, centralized educational web platform specifically designed for Algerian high school students (1st, 2nd, and 3rd year / Baccalaureate). It provides structured access to lessons, detailed exercises, summaries, past exams, and curated YouTube video links—all tailored to the official Algerian curriculum and modernized with a sleek, Arabic-localized (RTL) interface.
